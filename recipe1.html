Fluffy Pancake Recipe 🥞

Soft, golden, and melt-in-your-mouth good — perfect for breakfast or anytime you need comfort on a plate.

🧾 Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir gently (don't overmix).
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat. Lightly grease it.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter onto pan. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve warm with honey, fruits, or syrup!

🍓 Best Served With:

  • Fresh strawberries, bananas or blueberries 🍌
  • Warm maple syrup or honey 🍯
  • A scoop of vanilla ice cream 🍦
  • Yoghurt and nuts 🥜
  • Glass of fresh orange juice 🧃

💪 Nutritional Benefits:

  • Rich in carbohydrates for energy
  • Contains proteins from eggs and milk
  • Good source of calcium for bones 🦴
  • Eggs provide essential vitamins like B12
  • Can be made healthier with oats or whole wheat flour
