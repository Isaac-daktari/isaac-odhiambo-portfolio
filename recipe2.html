Minced Meat & Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g minced meat (beef)
  • 1 pack spaghetti
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Tomato paste or fresh tomatoes
  • Salt, pepper, and seasoning

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti with a pinch of salt until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, saut√© onions and garlic until golden.
  3. Add minced meat, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes or tomato paste, simmer for 10 mins.
  5. Season to taste. Mix with spaghetti or serve side-by-side.

Nutritional Benefits

  • High in protein from beef
  • Good source of iron and B-vitamins
  • Energy-rich carbohydrates from pasta
  • Tomatoes provide antioxidants like lycopene
  • Can be enriched with veggies for fiber
